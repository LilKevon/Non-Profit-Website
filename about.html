The Start of A Dream

Programming is extremely difficult, even for the most innovative of minds. Kevin and Jeffery both knew this too well. When Kevin started out, he quited several times because he felt the costs of programming outweighed the rewards. When Jeffery started his programming journey, he found it to be quite difficult as well.

So when Kevin approached Jeffery with a idea, to guide future programmers in their journey through the realms of computer programming, Jeffery agreed to take on the task. Through countless hours of web development, class creation, and ensuring that Kevin's organization would reach its potential, Output("Success!") was created.

Now, together with Jeffery, Kevin believes that, through Output("Success!"), students can discover their passion within Computer Science, all with a click of a button.

Meet The Team!

Output("Success!") is a non-profit, youth-led organization dedicated to providing free tutoring services for students interested in Computer Programming. Created by Kevin Li, his team and him guarentee high quality and safe lessons that will give a great impression of Computer Science to your children, whether they are expirenced or just starting out.
