2005/01/24: Humble Beginnings

Hi, Kevin here! Today marks Output("Success!")'s grand opening! I'm really excited to bring this project to life! With my friend Jeffery, we have developed programs that are designed to aid students in learning the essence of Computer Programming. We worked really hard to bring these carefully thought-out programs to our community, and we hope that everyone will be pleased with what we have created.


Since we just started out, there are a few things I would like to mention. As a new organization, we encourage constructive criticism from parents and students. We want to keep improving our programs, and any input is welcome! If you would like to submit feedback, just click the Contact link at the top, and send a email to our address. You can also send a email to one of our tutors!


Next, we are planning on expanding our programs if all goes well. It is well known that there are countless programming languages out there, and we want to try our best to bring great programs for all of them. However, we are sticking to Java as of right now. Keep on the lookout for new notifications in our blog!

Finally, please note that all classes are virtual. There is no in-person lesson, due to the COVID pandemic.


Thats it for this post! I hope that this goes well, and we continue developing in order to give students the programming education they deserve.
